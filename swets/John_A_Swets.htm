Bibliographic Sketch:  John A. Swets, Ph.D.             Chief Scientist (emer.), BBN Technologies;

                                                                                                Lecturer on Health Care Policy, Harvard

                                                                                                Medical School

Education:

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI                                          B.A.                                          1950                                          Psychology

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI                                          M.A.                                          1953                                          Psychology

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI                                          Ph.D.                                          1954                                          Psychology

 

Professional Experience

1954-56   Instructor in Psychology, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI

1956-63   Assistant to Associate Professor of Psychology, Massachusetts Institute of Technology

1964-70   Director of the Information Science and Technology Division and Vice President, Bolt

                 Beranek and Newman, Inc.

1971-74   Senior Vice Present and General Manager, Bolt Beranek and Newman, Inc.

1975-98   Chief Scientist, Bolt Beranek and Newman, Inc.

1985-88   Lecturer on Clinical Epidemiology, Harvard Medical School

1989-       Lecturer on Health Care Policy, Harvard Medical School

1997-       Senior Research Associate in Radiology, Brigham and Women’s Hospital
