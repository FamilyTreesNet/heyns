Heyns Roots

Four Heijns brothers who moved from the Netherlands to the U.S. and Canada and became Heyns.

From the Heijns Farm

Gerrit (b. 21 Nov 1824)
Branch of UC Berkeley Chancellor Roger Heyns (1918-1995)
Matthijs (b. 23 May 1827)
Branch of Grand Rapid grocer Matt Heyns, his son William and son Larry Heyns in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Larry's son Loren and Loren's kids Ethan and Abbey, live in Atlanta, Georgia.
Frederik (b. 25 Jul 1830)
Branch of Jennifer Heyns, in Canada.
Willem (b. 8 Oct 1832)
